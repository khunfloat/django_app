User Guide

1. Get our QRcode from our store.

2. Register your account and login. [Home] -> [Log in]

3. Activate your QRcode [Home] -> [My Packages] -> [Activate QRcode]

4. Register QRcode for the delivery information. [Home] -> [Register Package]

5. Fill out the information form. and submit

6. Glue a QRcode on your package and take it to drop station.

7. Track your package [Home] -> [Track]
